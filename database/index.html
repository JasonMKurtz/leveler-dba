THE DISTRIBUTION PROCESS: ** THIS IS ALMOST DONE being built on firebase ** If you are a salaried human or don’t feel like you’re at risk of losing your job this week or next, this site was made to make it easy for you to distribute a small portion of your wealth to our friends in the gig economy while we all make it through this extremely weird moment. This process will take a few minutes and is best done on your phone. Here’s how it works: By clicking a button, you will be assigned a “Group Number” You will then be directed to the database. Look for your group number in the database and distribute to that group only via their payment links. Please distribute evenly across that group. In other words, if you have decided you want to distribute 50 dollars, send each of your 10 assigned people $5.00 The database will cycle through, indefinitely. Distribute once, distribute weekly, distribute however much you want, but whenever you click the button, you will be assigned a list based on where the list is in the cycle. Suggested donation is $50.00 sum total. Venmoing 10 people 5 dollars each might feel inconsequential, but if we reach a critical mass, it will add up.
